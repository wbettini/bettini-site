# About Me

**Bill Bettini**  
*Data & AI Strategist | Enterprise Architecture & Analytics Leader | MSDAAI Candidate*

I’m a Data Architect and Product Manager with a proven track record of leading enterprise-scale data strategy, machine learning, and analytics initiatives that deliver measurable business impact. Over the past two decades, I’ve guided technology transformation at some of the world’s most respected financial institutions — including BNY Mellon, Bank of America, Deutsche Bank, and Lehman Brothers — aligning innovation with business priorities, governance, and client value.

Today, my focus is on bridging the gap between AI potential and business reality. I help organizations define the right problems to solve, architect scalable solutions, and select or develop the tools — whether AI, advanced analytics, or automation — that deliver sustainable results. My approach blends strategic vision with hands-on technical expertise, from enterprise data architecture and governance to advanced analytics with Power BI, Python, and cloud-scale platforms.

I’m currently advancing my expertise through an online Master of Science in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (MSDAAI) at Florida Gulf Coast University, building on my foundation in Computer Sciences and years of applied leadership in workflow & data product management, data governance, intelligent automation, and enterprise architecture.
